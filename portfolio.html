Designer of the Month

7th Avenue Jewellery
Year Started : 2017
Specialities : Womenswear, Bridal, Customisation, Accessories, Jewellery, Ready to Wear, Online
Founded by a young jewellery designer Ravina P, 7th avenue jewellery is a premium fashion jewellery line with a vision to deliver high quality semiprecious statement pieces. Ravina completed her degree from Fashion Institute of Technology, New York which is located on 7th Avenue. This led to the beginning of her jewellery journey and hence the brand name. Being a certified diamond grader from the International Gemmological Institute, Ravina is known for evaluating her stones and bringing the highest quality possible to her clients. The brand caters to a young generation of individualistic women with a passion for their work, who are socially active and who want to be free from handling expensive jewellery. The brand specializes in destination jewellery by using premier quality stones like cubic zirconia and other semi-precious stones set in silver alloy paired with vibrant colours. Available in multi-designer stores across the country, namely, Fuel (Mumbai), Agashe (New Delhi), Deval (Ahmedabad), Armadio (Bangalore), Palace Atelier (Jaipur), Saanja Fashion (Surat), etc and online through Amazon, Flipkart, confidress, Aashni&Co, to name a few. Each 7th Avenue piece is handcrafted by designers and craftsmen, narrating a story not only about its origin, but also about the women who wear them. Their vast collection is perfect for a luxurious destination wedding, cocktail party or a summer getaway with pieces ranging from bracelets, earrings, necklaces, rings and tikkas.
